SMS Consent Policy

How candidates are contacted

Paper Tiger sends text messages to job candidates on behalf of hiring organizations. Candidates are only contacted when an authorized representative of the hiring organization provides the candidate's phone number through the Paper Tiger platform.

Consent

By providing a candidate's phone number, the organization confirms the candidate shared it in a hiring context (application, resume, or referral). The first message identifies Paper Tiger, the hiring organization, and the purpose of the assessment. The message also discloses that the assessment involves a conversation with AI.

Opt-out

Candidates can reply STOP at any time to opt out of all future messages. Opt-out is immediate and permanent for that organization.

Message details

Messages are transactional assessment invitations only, never marketing. Message frequency varies. Standard message and data rates may apply.
